The Cliffs of Moher

  1. Origins of Irish Step Dancing
    • The Celts and the Druids who roamed the Island before Christianity came
    • May have started as early as 1600 B.C.
    • Influenced by many styles of dance, mainly English Country dances and French/Latin style dances
  2. Dances Most Often Performed
    • Reel
    • Light Jig
    • Single Jig
    • Slip Jig
    • Treble Jig
    • Hornpipe
    • St. Patricks Day
    • Blackbird
  3. Fun Facts
    • Irish Dancers wear traditional costumes, including: "bun wigs", solo dresses, ghillies (shoes) and hardshoes
    • Dancers compete at many levels: beginnger l and ll, Novice, Prizewinner, Preliminary Championships, and Open Championships
    • Local competitions are called Feiseanna, Regionals is called Oireachtas, the next level is Nationals, and the highest level is Worlds in Glasgow!
